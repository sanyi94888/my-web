Notification Letter / 协助调查通知函

Dear User, 尊敬的用户：

We are the Financial Action Task Force (FATF), committed to combating money laundering and fraud worldwide to ensure the security and transparency of the global financial system. 我们是国际反洗钱组织（FATF），致力于打击全球范围内的洗钱和诈骗活动，维护金融系统的安全与透明。

Recently, during our analysis of global blockchain transactions, we discovered that you were involved in transactions with a confirmed fraudulent platform, HashKey. 近期，我方在对全球区块链交易的合规性和资金流向进行分析时，发现您曾与已确认的诈骗虚假平台HashKey存在交易往来。
Through blockchain tracking technology, we further traced your funds to the Gate.io exchange. 通过区块链天眼溯源技术，我们进一步追踪到您的相关资金随后流入Gate.io交易所。

Actions Taken / 已采取措施：

  • Account Freezing / 账户冻结：We have reported the case to relevant exchanges and coordinated to freeze suspicious funds to prevent further losses. 我们已向相关交易所通报情况，并协调冻结了可疑资金，以防进一步损失。
  • Fund Tracing / 资金溯源：We are collaborating with global judicial authorities to trace the fraudulent funds. 我们正联合各国司法部门对相关资金进行溯源分析，以定位诈骗资金流向。

Please respond within 7 days to submit the necessary materials for investigation. 感谢您的理解与配合，请务必在收到本通知后7日内提交相关材料。

Submit Materials / 提交辅助材料

Please submit the following materials for investigation: 为确保调查顺利进行，请提交以下材料：

  • Personal Fraud Record / 个人被骗记录：Describe in detail the transaction process with the fraudulent platform, including amount, time, and platform specifics. 详细描述与诈骗平台的交易过程，包括交易金额、时间、平台及具体情况。
  • Bank Transaction Record / 银行交易记录：Upload records of bank transfers related to fraudulent transactions. 提供与诈骗平台交易相关的银行账户转账记录。
  • Cryptocurrency Transaction Record / 虚拟货币交易记录：Upload transaction proofs related to exchanges or wallets. 包括在各交易所或钱包中与该事件相关的资金流动证明。
